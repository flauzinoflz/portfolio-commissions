Commissions

REGION 03 // COM

Status: open

I take commissions for digital illustration and concept art — from character portraits to environment and prop design. Every project starts with a conversation: you describe the idea, I reply with scope, timeline and price.

Available styles

  • Character illustration — bust, half body or full body
  • Character, creature and prop concept art
  • Environment concept art and keyframes
  • Covers and promotional art (games, books, music)

Terms

  • 50% upfront, 50% on final delivery
  • Up to two revision rounds included at sketch stage
  • Timelines set per project — 2 to 4 weeks on average
  • Commercial use negotiated separately; personal use included
  • Not accepted: hateful content, plagiarism of living artists, NFT

How it works

  • Brief — you send references and a description of the idea
  • Proposal — scope, timeline and budget agreed in writing
  • Sketch — composition approved before final rendering
  • Delivery — high-resolution file + web-ready versions

Contact

Send your idea by email with the subject “Commission”. I reply within 48 business hours.
